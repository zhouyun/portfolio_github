Design-it-yourself maze

Individual project, December 2013

  • Design makes a better world, first, my world.

    As a designer, I design something just for myself from time to time. If other people also like it, jockpot!

    I gave the maze to a friend as a gift. I enjoyed creating it, and the joy doubled for sharing.

  • I love maze.

    Do you remember Cobb asked Ariadne to design him a maze in two minutes? No way you can solve it. Yeah, I have to admit that I am that kind of person who paused the movie just to try to solve the maze instead of grasping an ice cream. You can imagine how exciting I am when I decided to make my own maze.

    image from Inception

  • Sustainability is more fun.

    To share the joy of maze design, I created the maze with movable pieces. People were invited to move the pieces to challenge the next player, and people love to play maze in this way.

  • Ending like this? Not enough.

    I made a 2.5-dimension maze with acrylic. Despite of wires and controller, it was more about chaos of shapes. Watching people interacting with it was great fun.
